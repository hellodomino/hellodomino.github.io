WHO WE ARE

DOMINO IS A NON PROFIT CHARITY ORGANIZATION WORKING ON EDUCATION IN ORPHANAGES AND EVERY SOCIACULTURAL ASPECTS OF THE LIVES OF THE THOUSANDS OF ORPHANS WE ASSIST, WE PROMOT VOLUNTEER WORKING AND BUILD SOLIDARITY CHAINS AMONGST YOUTHS BECAUSE WE BELIEVE IN THEM.


BUILDING AND IMPROVING THE LIVES AND LIVELIHOODS OF SOME OF THE WORLD’S POOREST AND MOST DISADVANTAGED PEOPLE.

FOR THE LAST 6 YEARS WE HAVE PROVIDED PROFESSIONAL EDUCATION AND VOLONTEER WORKING ON A SUSTAINED AND LASTING BASIS, AND TODAY WE REPRESENT A SUSTAINABLE SOLUTION FOR THE EDUCATION AND WELFARE OF THOUSANDS OF ORPHANS. SINCE 2015, DOMINO TEAMS HAVE BEEN WORKING THROUGHOUT DOUALA TO HELP MORE THAN 1000 ORPHANS AND STREET CHILDREN. WE NOW BUILD, AND RUN EFFICIENTLY EDUCATION PROGRAMS IN ORPHANAGES AND ACCOMODATION CENTERS BECAUSE GIVING THEM EDUCATION IS GIVING THEM A BRIGHT FUTURE.
